Game Developer Distribution (by Percent) Worldwide 2014-2021, by Gender:

The following visualization displays the percent distribution of game developers by gender. It is clear through this visualization that people who identify as a gender besides male are significantly underrepresented in the game developer space as they have made up at least 61% of the game developer positions since 2014.

To view the specific percentages from each year you can move your cursor across the graph.

Average Salaries in the Gaming Industry Worldwide 2018:

This visualization depicts the average salaries of employees within the gaming industry worldwide, in 2018. Both the mean and median salaries are shown for each gender, which was found through the Game Industry Careers Survey. Both the mean and median salaries are higher for males than they are for females, with the mean salary being 46,700 for females and the mean salary being 51,750 for males.
