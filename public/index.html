IN GAMING

 Report Resources Citations

Gender Gap In Gaming aims to explore and showcase the similarities and disparities for women in gaming both within the industry and in player bases.

Our mission is to provide resources for aspiring women who want to take part in the gaming space, but feel hesitant or intimidated by the current culture that gaming has.
